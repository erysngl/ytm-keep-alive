YTM Keep-Alive

A pure DOM, extension-free bookmarklet that prevents YouTube Music from pausing automatically. 100% safe and bypasses TrustedHTML restrictions.

Drag to Bookmarks
How to install:
1. Click and hold the red button above.
2. Drag it to your browser's Bookmarks Bar.
3. Open YouTube Music and click the new bookmark to activate!

If your bookmarks bar is hidden, press Ctrl + Shift + B
